Who we are

EduPort is a course registration platform that helps learners enroll in skill-based courses and summer camps. We offer soft skills and IT courses for everyone, and a summer camp program exclusively for students in grades 7 to 12. Create an account, browse offerings, and register in a few clicks. Your registrations are reviewed by our team—we’ll confirm your spot once approved.

Choose from skill-based courses or our summer camp. Sign in to browse and register. All programs require an account and admin approval after you register.
